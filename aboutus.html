About HelloDoctor

Your health is our mission. We're here to make healthcare simple, fast, and accessible. Whether you're booking an appointment or checking your health updates, we bring medical care closer to you.

Fun Fact & History

Did you know? HelloDoctor was born as a university project in 2023. It is just an idea among friends who wanted to make healthcare easier to access.

What started with simple appointment bookings soon turned into a complete health companion in your pocket. Today, HelloDoctor is helping people stay healthy, informed, and connected with doctors – all from their phones!

Our Mission

To provide healthcare that is simple, fast, and accessible for everyone. We want people to manage their health easily from anywhere.

Our Vision

To become a trusted healthcare platform that helps people stay healthy using modern technology and support.

What Makes Us Unique

  • 24/7 online appointment booking
  • Friendly, multilingual staff
  • Over 95% patient satisfaction
  • Modern diagnostic equipment

Why Choose HelloDoctor?

From the comfort of your home, HelloDoctor connects you with certified doctors you can trust. Here’s why thousands of patients choose us:

  • 24/7 appointment access
  • Zero waiting time with online booking
  • Transparent reviews and ratings
  • Multilingual support for diverse communities
  • Easy to use - even for first-time users!
